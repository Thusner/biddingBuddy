Course content 
Inequality has become one of the most discussed subjects in the scientific world, but also in the political sphere. This seminar explores the various aspects of inequality and equality. It provides the students with an overview of theories, concepts and measurement problems of inequality. A special emphasis will be given on special aspects such as gender, migration, race, intergenerational aspects, health and education. Also classical subjects like income and wealth will be discussed. Also the consequences of the Corona Pandemic will be adressed. 
The course is organized as a block course. It starts with an introductory session, where the course outline, the requirements and the literature will be explained. 
Further sessions deal with specific subjects of inequality and equality, starting with basic concepts, definitions and measurement problems. Inequality will be handled from different perspectives and disciplines. It includes, the importance of the Welfare State for equality, inequality and education, inequality and health, gender equality, migration and other subjects.
